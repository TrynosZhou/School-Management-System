# Computer Science Test (CS-0478-01)
Class: 4 Blue
Total Marks: 75 (assembled: 75)

1. (10 marks) Convert 0.375 to a fraction in simplest form.

2. (5 marks) Find the area of a rectangle with length 12 cm and width 7 cm.

3. (10 marks) Construct a frequency table from the following scores: 4, 6, 6, 7, 8, 8, 8, 9, 10, 10.

4. (10 marks) A shop gives a discount of 15% on a shirt marked at GH¢200. Find the selling price.

5. (5 marks) Solve for x: 2(x - 3) = 10.

6. (10 marks) The ratio of boys to girls in a class is 3:5. If there are 24 girls, how many students are in the class?

7. (10 marks) Expand and simplify: (x + 2)(x − 5).

8. (5 marks) Plot the point P(−2, 3) on the Cartesian plane and reflect it in the y-axis. State the coordinates of the image.

9. (5 marks) Evaluate: 3/4 + 5/6, giving your answer in simplest form.

10. (5 marks) Simplify: 3x + 5x - 2x.
